# Prompt Practice

## 1. Tournament Announcement

### Prompt:

Write an exciting [e-game] announcement under 100 words.
Winner: Team 1
MVP: Player 1

### Output:

THE CHAMPIONS HAVE BEEN CROWNED! 🏆

After a grueling season of electrifying plays, heart-stopping clutches, and pure domination, Team 1 has officially secured the championship title! They brought the heat and left it all in the arena, proving they are the undisputed kings of the server.

A massive shoutout goes to the tournament MVP, Player 1, who pulled off some of the most insane, game-changing moves we’ve ever witnessed. Your legacy is officially cemented!

Congratulations, Team 1! 🎮🔥

### Observation:

Including the winner and MVP details produced a personalized and more engaging announcement.

## 2. Tournament Rules

### Prompt:

Generate 5 professional [e-game] tournament rules for competitive players.

### Output:

Official Competitive Tournament Rules
1. Punctuality & Attendance: Teams must check in 30 minutes prior to the scheduled match time. Failure to appear in the lobby within 15 minutes of the start time results in an automatic match forfeit.

2. Competitive Integrity: The use of any unauthorized third-party software, hardware macros, or intentional in-game exploits is strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate disqualification and ban.

3. Code of Conduct: Players must maintain professional sportsmanship at all times. Toxic behavior, hate speech, collusion, or match-fixing will lead to immediate team disqualification and forfeiture of prizing.

4. Disconnection & Pauses: Each team is permitted a maximum of 5 minutes of pause time per game solely for verifiable technical or connectivity issues. Tournament admins must be notified immediately upon pausing.

5. Roster Validity: Only players officially registered on the team roster prior to the tournament lock are permitted to compete. Utilizing unregistered substitutes or "ringers" will result in immediate disqualification.

### Observation:

The AI generated clear and organized rules suitable for a tournament environment.

## 3. Welcome Message

### Prompt:

Write a welcome message for new users joining an esports tournament platform.

### Output:

Welcome to the Arena, Challenger! 🎮
We are absolutely thrilled to have you join our esports community. Whether you’re here to sweat it out in high-stakes tournaments, climb the leaderboards, or just play some casual brackets with friends, you’ve officially found your new competitive home.

This platform was built by gamers, for gamers, to give you the seamless tournament experience you deserve. No more messy spreadsheets or bracket confusion—just pure, adrenaline-pumping competition.

🚀 3 Quick Steps to Get You Game-Ready:
1. Link Your Accounts: Head over to your profile settings to connect your Discord, game IDs (Steam, Riot, Epic, etc.), and socials. This ensures smooth lobby invites and automatic stat tracking.

2. Scout the Competition: Browse our Tournaments Tab to find upcoming events for your favorite titles. Filter by region, skill tier, or prize pool to find your perfect match.

3. Form Your Squad: Competing in a team game? Create a team profile, invite your friends, and lock in your roster before registration closes.

💡 Pro-Tip: Make sure to join our official Discord server. It’s the best place to find scrim partners, grab quick support from tournament admins, and catch live announcements.

Your journey to the top of the leaderboard starts right now. Gear up, practice hard, and above all else...

Good Luck, Have Fun! 👑

### Observation:

The AI produced a friendly and encouraging message suitable for onboarding users.

# Overall Learning

Through these experiments, I observed that providing more context, constraints, and specific details in a prompt results in more relevant, personalized, and professional AI-generated outputs. This demonstrated the importance of prompt engineering in controlling the quality and usefulness of AI responses.
